Learn More About Concordia Prep

List of 15 items.

  • Mission Statement

    Concordia Preparatory School is a community devoted to excellent education, leadership, and Christian service.

  • Core Values

    Faith, Integrity, Service, Leadership, and Community

  • Statement of Diversity and Inclusion

    The Office of Diversity and Inclusion aims to create a diverse and inclusive community for students, faculty, staff, and parents. This means defining diversity and inclusion for the Concordia Prep family, as well as creating opportunities for important discussions, learning opportunities, and activities about ethnic and cultural differences.

  • Enrollment

    440+, grades 9-12 - 300+, grades 6-8 – 140

  • Founded

    In 1965 as the Baltimore Lutheran School. Renamed the Concordia Preparatory School in 2014.

  • Religious Affiliation

    Lutheran Church Missouri-Synod.  CPS welcomes students from all backgrounds and religions.

  • Faith Information

    • Campus Ministry – Pastor Peter Couser
    • Partnership with NewThing Church
    • Religious Studies
    • Weekly Chapel
    • Faith Retreats
    • Fellowship of Christian Athletes
    • Middle School Meetups
    • Service Learning

  • Academics

    • Honors and AP Courses offered each year: Honors: 15-20; AP: 10-12
    • CPS STEAM Program: Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math
    • Foreign Language Program: Spanish, Latin, & German
    • NEW FOR FALL 2023 – ACADEMIC PATHWAYS: Health Science, Advanced Mathematics, and Honors

  • Academic Pathways Program

    Academically, Concordia Preparatory School offers a competitive college preparatory program. For students who enjoy increased rigor, the school encourages participation in pathways designed to prepare students for future careers in specific fields.The Program is being launched in Fall 2023 in the following subject areas:
    1. Health Science Pathway
    2. Advanced Mathematics Pathway
    3. Honors Pathway

     

  • Nationally Accredited

    Maryland State Department of Education, Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools

  • College Matriculation

    • 97-99% of CPS seniors attend 4 or 2-year colleges. 
    • Class of 2023 received over $4 million+ dollars in scholarships.

  • Athletics

    The Concordia Prep SAINTS play in the IAAM and MIAA Leagues. 
    • Colors are RED and GRAY.
    • MOTTO: All to the Glory of God
    • 19 Upper School teams – Varsity and JV: baseball, basketball, cross country, football, lacrosse, soccer, softball, track and field, and volleyball
    • 11 Middle School teams: baseball, basketball, cross country, flag football, lacrosse, soccer, softball, track and field, and volleyball.
    • 7 championships in the last five years.
    • 77% of the student body participates in athletics.

  • Fine and Performing Arts

    80% of students participate in the Arts: Art, Chorus, Band, Chapel Band and Theatre – Variety Show, Fall Play, Middle and Upper School Plays, and Spring Musical

  • Technology

    • Student BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) program
    • Interactive classroom technology
    • Upgraded technology infrastructure, including campus-wide wireless Internet access.
    • Blackbaud Education Management Solutions learning and communication platform for students, parents, and teachers.
    • Microsoft Office 365 suite and Google for Education productivity applications. 

  • Location/Campus

    Concordia Prep is nestled in a quiet neighborhood on 25 acres in Towson, MD, conveniently off the beltway featuring: Upper School Building, Middle School Building, Lutheran Activities Center (LAC) – gymnasium, weight room, black box theatre, music room, and cafeteria, Turf Field, and Upper/Practice Field.

Towson, Maryland, 21286
