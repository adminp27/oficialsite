The Office of College Counseling and Guidance

Concordia Prep School is concerned with the well-being of the whole student. The Guidance Office and Campus Pastor assist students and their families with individual and group counseling. The Academic Dean/College Counselor provides group and individual career interest planning and college counseling to students throughout their time at Concordia Prep.
 
All juniors meet with the College Counselor individually and in groups to prepare them for the college application process. The Guidance Office hosts colleges on campus for students to meet individual college recruiters and organizes a college information night with admissions officers and financial aid professionals to explain the admissions and financial aid process for students applying to college.
 
The Guidance Office administers the PSAT to eighth, ninth, tenth, and eleventh-grade students, AP exams, and standardized tests to Middle School students.
