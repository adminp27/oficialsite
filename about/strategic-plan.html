Strategic Plan

In August of 2023, the Concordia Prep Board of Directors enacted a Strategic Plan to guide the school's future over the next five years. This plan identifies challenges and provides a framework to leverage opportunities in all areas but most especially in the school's commitment to students’ spiritual, academic, physical, and social growth.

List of 6 items.

  • Build to Stay Competitive and Expand Mission: Facility Expansion and Improvement

    • Study the feasibility of a building project for additional classrooms, a weight room, an auditorium, and other necessary campus improvements.
    • Study the feasibility of a capital campaign to raise money for any necessary expansion and improvements.
    • Develop plan to improve maintenance and appearance of existing facilities.

  • Supporting Programs to Stay Competitive and Expand Mission: Educational Excellence

    • Continued improvement of faith enriching activities and intentional development with CPS’s core values.
    • Expand programs to further challenge high achieving students.
    • Expand support for students struggling academically.
    • Study and expand future curricular opportunities for students.
    • Secure resources to recruit and retain an outstanding and diverse faculty and staff.
    • Secure resources to support CPS’s talented faculty and staff as they grow as educators.
    • Continued development of CPS’s co-curricular programs to enhance student growth.

  • Supporting Programs to Stay Competitive and Expand Mission: Maximize Community Connection and Outreach

    • Study and improve alumni connection and events.
    • Develop a CPS community business directory.

  • Supporting Programs to Stay Competitive and Expand Mission: Marketing and Communications

    • Develop plan to improve public awareness of Concordia Preparatory School.
    • Improve communication plan for current families and alumni.

  • Supporting Programs to Stay Competitive and Expand Mission: Improve Financial Capabilities

    • Develop plan to improve existing revenue streams and develop new revenue opportunities.
    • Grow CPS endowments to better support our ministry.
    • Improve advancement strategies and events to maximize support for the ministry at CPS.

  • Supporting Programs to Stay Competitive and Expand Mission: Maximize CPS Student Body

    • Develop strategies to continue to recruit the best students to CPS.
    • Recruit additional international students.
    • Establish Saints Ambassador Program.
